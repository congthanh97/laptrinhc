   Tap tin TC.ZIP la tap tin nen toan bo thu muc Turbo C version 2.0.bang 
Norton Commander.
   Ban cung dung Norton Commander version 5.0 de bung tap tin nay, hoan
nguyn lai cac tap tin cua phan mem Tubo C. 
   Ban lan luot thuc hien nhu sau:
   1-Tao thu muc TC tren dia C: hoac D:
	-Bang DOS go lenh MD \TC bam <Enter>
   	-Bang Norton 
	   +Bam phim <F7>
	   +Go ten thu muc TC
	   +Bam phim <Enter>
   2-Mo 2 khung panels: Mot ben la o dia A:, mot ben la thu muc TC vua 
tao trn dia cung.
   3-Di vet sang (Hilight) den tap tin TC.ZIP tren dia A:,bam phim <Insert>
de chon
   4-Bam dong thoi 2 phim <Alt + F6>. Di con tro den muc Include 
subdirectories, bam phim <Enter> 2 lan. 
   Norton Commander se hoan nguyen lai cac tap tin
